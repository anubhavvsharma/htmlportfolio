Best movies of all time

My top 3 best movies

Parasite

This movies revolves around a poor unemployed family.
Soon, all of them find a way to work within the same household and start living a parasitic life.

Fight Club

Unhappy with his capitalistic lifestyle, a white-collared insomniac forms an underground fight club with Tyler, a careless soap salesman.
Soon, their venture spirals down into something sinister.

Catch Me If You Can

Notorious con artist Frank Abagnale has duped people worth millions of dollars with his masterful art of deception.
With his scams getting bolder, he is soon pursued by FBI agent Carl Hanratty.
